100 Footer is delectable average cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Built with 45ml vodka and 15ml bitters plum with 110ml orange juice as well as 130ml ice and served with or for breakfast. Just place ice into glass then splash in plum bitters into glass over ice and add ane and a half shots of vodka. Pour in orange juice and served in a goblet glass cold.
