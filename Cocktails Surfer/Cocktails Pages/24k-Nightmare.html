24k Nightmare is delectable super strong cocktail at 2.1 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml peppermint liqueur and 15ml rum 151 proof with 15ml goldwasser liqueur as well as 15ml jagermeister and best with an entree or small snack. Just two shots always share a shot then add all ingredients to shaker and quick dry shake without ice. Pour into glass and served in a shot glass at room temperature.
